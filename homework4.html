Cancer Death Rates in Different Countries

Relationship between type of cancer and nationality

Posted by Emily Cheung on March 10, 2017
 

Cancer is a group of diseases involving abnormal cell growth with the potential to invade or spread to other parts of the body. There were an estimated 13 thousand cancer mortality in Hong Kong in 2014, according to the Hong Kong Cancer Registry under Hospital Authority.The nationality is observed to be a factor of getting certain type of cancer. It is believed that external factors, such as eating habits, living environment, and the working style would affect the cancer death rates.
